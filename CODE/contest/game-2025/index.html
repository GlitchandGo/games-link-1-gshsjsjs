Community Game Challenge 2025

A celebration of ingenuity, technical skill, and playful imagination — unite creators from across the globe to build interactive magic.

An Invitation to Visionaries

To every dreamer, builder, and pixel‑level perfectionist: This is more than a competition — it is a call to craft an experience that will outlive its own code. Whether your medium is the vibrant stage of Scratch or the infinite canvas of HTML5, you are invited to make your mark.

Competition Details

Theme Original interactive games in Scratch or HTML5, or conversions to HTML5.
Prize $30 USD for the top selection, only if at least 25 valid entries are received.
Entry Period 9/1/2025 – 9/25/2025
Judging Criteria Creativity, usability, replay value, and execution.
Submission Link Submit Your Entry

Design Guidance

Not a checklist, but prompts to guide and inspire.

  • Surprise your player with an unexpected mechanic or twist.
  • Design for replay: what makes someone return?
  • Balance simplicity and depth — easy to start, hard to master.
  • Infuse personality through art, sound, or dialogue.

Rules & Eligibility

  1. Entries must be original and created during the contest period.
  2. Only Scratch or HTML5 projects are accepted.
  3. No offensive, hateful, or discriminatory content targeting any person or group.
  4. We reserve the right to disqualify any entry for any reason, at our sole discretion.
  5. We may cancel or stop the contest at any time and withhold prize money.
  6. By submitting, you transfer full ownership of your game to the contest organizers. You may not host, share, or distribute it elsewhere.
  7. We may use, modify, distribute, and display your game in any way, without limitation.
  8. Any borrowed assets must have proper permissions and credits.
  9. One entry per participant.
