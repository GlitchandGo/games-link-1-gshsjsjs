Competition Details

Theme Original interactive games in Scratch or HTML5

Submission Link Submit Your Entry Here

Design Guidance

Not a checklist, but a constellation of prompts to guide and inspire.

  • Surprise your player with an unexpected mechanic or narrative twist.
  • Design for replay: what makes someone return a second, third, or tenth time?
  • Balance simplicity and depth — the best games are easy to start, hard to master.
  • Infuse personality through art, sound, dialogue, or all three.

Rules & Eligibility

  1. Entries must be original and created during the contest period.
  2. Only projects built in Scratch or HTML5 will be accepted.
  3. Any borrowed assets must have appropriate permissions and credits.
  4. One entry per participant — make it count.
